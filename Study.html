Study Advice

Much of advice people give about studying is wrong.

Always Study in the same place?

Wrong!

Studying one topic intensivly before going on to the next?

Wrong!

here are some study tips that are based on actual research.

From Forget What You Know About Good Study habbits,By Benedict Carey, Published In The New York Times On September 6,2020.

Alternate Study Enviroments

Study in the library,in the coffee shop,in your room,....Varying the place you study helps you remember more

Mix Content

Studying a bit of this,a bit of that,some this again.some of the other,switching contents help remember more.

Space Study Session

Three one hour Sessions is more effective than one-three hour session.

Self Testing

Test Yourself,have a buddy do it.It helps you know how well you know.Coredog is good at this.
